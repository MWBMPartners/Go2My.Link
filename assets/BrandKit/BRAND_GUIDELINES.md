# Brand Guidelines ✅

## Preferred logo choices
- **Product UI (apps/dashboards):** `logos/optimized/Go2My.link-Logo-Optimized.svg`
- **Marketing/hero:** `logos/svg/Go2My.link-Logo-Glass.svg` (or PNG in `logos/png/`)
- **Dark backgrounds:** `logos/svg/Go2My.link-Logo-Dark.svg` or `logos/animated/*AutoTheme*.svg`
- **Print/1-colour:** `logos/svg/Go2My.link-Logo-Monochrome.svg`

## Clear space (safe area)
Keep at least **½ the icon height** of clear space around the logo.

## Do ✅
- Keep proportions unchanged
- Use provided colors (see `docs/`)
- Prefer SVG on web for crisp scaling

## Don't ❌
- Add outlines/shadows not provided
- Stretch/skew the logo
- Place on noisy backgrounds without contrast

## Color palette (suggested)
- Blue: `#1E88E5`
- Green: `#43A047`
- Slate: `#5A5F6A`
- Dark background: `#0A0E14`
